Hi there!

Nice to meet you! Here, I'm trying to list a few things I do. Most of it has to do with either programming, music, or entrepreneurship.

Work

I've been working at Official.fm since March 2011. We are a growing start-up with offices in Geneva, New York and San Francisco, looking to provide solutions to music professionals (artists, indie labels) in order to optimize their day-to-day.

Compilers

In 2009, I started creating a programming language called ooc. In the start inspired by Java but without the dumb, it ended up with more features than I expected, and as a result, the toolchain is sometimes less reliable then we wish it was. However I still haven't found a prettier language. After all, beauty is in the eye of the beholder.

Games

As a kid, I messed around a lot with game creation software from Clickteam: first The Games Factory, then Multimedia Fusion. I spent my high school years prototyping dozens of games, never finishing any, while writing music samples in Cakewalk 3.

Studies

In 2008 I started a Bachelor in micro-engineering at EPFL, Switzerland. Fortunately, it took me only one year to realize I wanted to study Computer Science instead. I've been working towards obtaining a master in CS ever since, on top of my job and demanding hobbies.

Music

I have been playing the piano for as long as I can remember. My favorite classical composers are Chopin, Rachmaninoff, and Liszt. Recently I've started a band with two friends where I sing and occasionally play the guitar and piano. We have no big releases as of yet!
